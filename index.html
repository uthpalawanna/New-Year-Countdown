Welcome to 2026 Animation

This interactive animation counts down to the year 2026 using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The countdown displays days, hours, minutes, and seconds remaining until January 1, 2026. You can start the countdown, reset it, or trigger a celebration animation for 2026. The background features dynamic particle effects that respond to mouse movement, creating an immersive visual experience.

As we approach 2026, the animation intensifies with color changes and particle acceleration. When the countdown reaches zero, a spectacular celebration with confetti and fireworks begins automatically.
